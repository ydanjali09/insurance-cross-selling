 📊 **Insurance Cross-Selling Analysis**
---

🚀 **Project Overview**
This project analyzes customer data to identify potential opportunities for **cross-selling insurance products**.  
The main goal is to understand customer behavior, identify high-potential segments, and suggest business strategies for increasing insurance conversions.

---

🎯**Objectives**
- Identify customers most likely to purchase additional insurance products  
- Analyze factors influencing conversion  
- Provide actionable business insights to improve marketing strategies  

---

🛠️ **Tools & Technologies**
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  

---

📂 **Dataset Features**
The dataset includes customer-related information such as:  
- Age, Income, Family Members  
- Gender, Marital Status  
- Education, Occupation, Job Title  
- Current Product & Product Type  
- New Product Type  
- Customer Rating (Cold, Warm, Hot)  
- Current and New Coverage Amount  
- Conversion Status (Purchased / Not Purchased)  

---

📊 **Analysis Performed**

 1. Univariate Analysis
- Distribution of numerical variables (Age, Income, Coverage)  
- Count analysis of categorical variables (Gender, Education, Occupation, Rating, etc.)  

🔹 2. Bivariate Analysis
- Numerical variables vs Conversion using boxplots  
- Categorical variables vs Conversion using countplots  
- Identification of patterns affecting purchase decisions  

 3. Multivariate Analysis
- Correlation heatmap to study relationships between numeric variables  
- Conversion rate comparison across multiple categories  

---

🔍 **Key Insights**

- Customers aged **35–50** show higher conversion rates  
- Income range **3–9 lakhs** is more likely to purchase insurance  
- Customers with **2–5 family members** are key targets  
- **Married individuals** have higher purchase probability  
- **Educated customers** are more aware and responsive  
- **SPT & SE occupations** show strong conversion trends  
- **Hot-rated customers** have the highest likelihood of purchase  
- Existing customers are ideal for **cross-selling strategies**  

---

 💡 **Business Recommendations**

- 🎯 Focus marketing on mid-age, high-income customers  
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Prioritize families with 2–5 members  
- 🔥 Target hot and warm leads for higher conversion  
- 💼 Promote relevant products to existing customers  
- ⚠️ Avoid aggressive selling to low-interest customers
